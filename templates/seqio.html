About FASTQ

FASTQ format stores nucleotide sequences with quality scores (Phred scores).

  • Phred Score: Quality indicator (higher = better)
  • Q20: 1% error rate
  • Q30: 0.1% error rate
  • Q40: 0.01% error rate
